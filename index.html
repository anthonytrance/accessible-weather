          Preferences and data

          Locations and preferences stay in this browser. Coordinates go to the listed weather providers. With alerts enabled, the server stores the chosen place and language. No account or profile.

          About data and sources

          Sources

          • Open-Meteo: forecasts, ensembles, model comparison and ERA5 reanalysis.
          • Buienradar.nl: two-hour rain nowcast.
          • KMI/IRM: Belgian station observations.
          • NOAA Aviation Weather Center: worldwide airport observations (METAR), via NOAA.
          • MET Norway: radar rain nowcast for the Nordic region.
          • Open-Meteo Air Quality: air quality and pollen (CAMS).
          • Location search data by GeoNames through Open-Meteo.

          Weather can change quickly. Use official warnings for safety-critical decisions.
